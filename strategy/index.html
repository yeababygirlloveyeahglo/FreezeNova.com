Hero Rush Tower Defense

For a fantastic, intense strategy game full of thrilling action, Hero Rush Tower Defense is here to take you away from the stresses of everyday life. Take the helm and protect your kingdom by defending it from enemies. With the help of 12 heroic characters, each bringing their own unique abilities to the table, you’ll be able to protect your citizens from the dangers that approach. Build a better defense strategy by upgrading your army and reinforcing your towers, all while boosting power in the upgrade section, buying special items in the store and using game-specifics from the guide section that cover tips to thwart enemies and more.

Kingdom Attack

What can you do when your kingdom is under attack? Well, stand and fight of course! With Kingdom Attack, you’ll be able to play through 120 levels with the assistance of alchemists and archers, to fight through an array of foes including dragons in an exciting adventure game that will keep you on your toes. You'll be able to not only protect your own kingdom, but take out others that are threatening the lives of your citizens.

Stick Guys Defense

When survival is the name of the game, Stick Guys Defense can be the top strategy title for your needs. Your kingdom needs to be defended in a fantastically designed winter wonderland where you’ll be able to upgrade your units and fight for survival to be the last kingdom standing. This differs from other strategy games in the sense that it is a strategy clicker, so you'll need to upgrade fast as your AI opponents get even cleverer. There are a host of boosters to be collected and exciting, intuitive gameplay that you simply can't miss.

Fantasy Tower Defense

If you're looking for a strategy game with a difference, Fantasy Tower Defense has 36 fantastic colourful cartoon levels that will challenge you like no other. The less you know about this title, the better as guessing is the name of the game so you'll really have to think about your strategies and the progression to come out on top best way to defend yourself is by clicking on the signs, but aside from this will leave it up to you. War Simulator

If you’re bored of the popular castle defense games, it’s time to take things back to zero, line up your men and battle for glory with War Simulator! With simplistic cartoon graphics and intuitive gameplay, you can beat your foes through 10 levels and three environments - all by selecting your formation and watching them go.

Zombie War Defence

Kicking things up a notch now, we're going from cartoony fun strategy games and throwing you into the deep end with cute but scary zombies with Zombie War Defense. How fast can you click and shoot as the zombie hoard approaches? With 30 levels of single player gameplay, the unique mix of human and animal zombies will come at you with increasing levels of speed as you go along. There are bonus attacks with cooldowns and you can upgrade your soldiers to ensure that these beasts don't get the better of you.

Arena of Heroes

Start your strategy game from the ground up with this epic gaming title Arena of Heroes. You'll have the ability to start your village and build it up as you go, train nothing short of the strongest warriors and invade surrounding villages to get your hands on the spoils of war (in this case, gold and elixir). With real-time gameplay management, your troops will need to collect elixir as you fight your enemies, to build strength. Will you be ready to go back to your king victorious?

Colony Simulator

If you want to do more than simply point and click with your strategy game, Colony Simulator could be a fantastic choice. The aim of the game is to build a thriving colony and as the leader, it will be your job to allocate resources, manage construction and expand your land with survival and prosperity in mind. One of the great things about this title is that each character in your charge will have their own health bar and you'll need to maintain their happiness and their hunger, too. There’s an array of special skills to help you do just this, including farming, cooking, logging, hunting, fighting and mining. You will even be able to meet with traders to help in your efforts. Leader beware however, as these will not always have the best intentions for your people.

Inventory sections give you the unique ability to see who is performing tasks as they should and you can delegate them to different ones if they’re not. There are zones, base buildings, gather buildings and factory buildings, as well as an economy and tech section that will give you key information to move throughout the game. It really doesn't get more comprehensive than this!
