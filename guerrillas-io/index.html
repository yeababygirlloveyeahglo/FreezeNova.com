Guerrillas.io

By FreezeNova

Take action as a member of an elite team in Guerrillas.io, where every second counts, and every move could mean victory or defeat. This online multiplayer FPS lets you dive into intense battles, with game maps inspired by the legendary Counter-Strike series. Whether you’re leading your team to victory in Team Deathmatch, or fighting solo in Free for All, you’ll need to bring your A-game.

Get Started

  • You can jump right into the fray by joining an existing room or creating your own.
  • The "Rooms" section on the menu allows you to see available battles or set up your own, giving you control over the number of players, game mode, objectives, bots, and even the map.
  • Customization is key in Guerrillas.io—you can tweak your loadout in the "Armoury," grab game-changing perks, and even customize the taunts your enemies see when you take them down.

Controls

  • Move: WASD keys or Arrow keys
  • Fire: Left mouse click
  • Aim: Right mouse click
  • Jump: Space key
  • Slide: Q key
  • Run: Left Shift
  • Switch Weapons: 1, 2, 3
  • Interact/Pick Up: E or F key
  • Throw Grenades: G key
  • Use Medkit: H key
  • Chat: T key
  • Toggle Cursor Lock: L key
  • Crouch: C key
  • Show Scoreboard: Hold Tab
  • Pause: ESC

Game Modes

  • Team Deathmatch: Work with your team to take down the opposition in a battle of strategy and skill.
  • Free for All: Go solo and rely on your instincts to be the last one standing.
  • Capture the Flag: Engage in strategic team play to steal the enemy’s flag and secure it at your base.
  • Gun Game: Start with a basic weapon and upgrade as you earn victories.
  • Arcade Doom: Unleash your inner warrior with invincibility, powerful guns, and grenade launchers, all are unlocked.

Customization and Perks

  • The Armoury is your hub for all things weapons and gear.
  • Choose from a variety of arms like the MP5K, CZ75, or even a trusty knife, and enhance your combat effectiveness with armor and helmets.
  • Equip perks like Quickdraw Ammo for faster reloads, UAV Ghost to avoid detection, or Bomber Man to rain explosives on your foes.
  • You can also add a personal touch by customizing your kill messages or even showing off with emotes like the Macarena Dance or Robot Hip Hop Dance.
