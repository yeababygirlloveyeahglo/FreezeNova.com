How to Play Puppet Master?

Puppet Master is straightforward: click the play button, and your puppet target appears, ready for whatever you throw at him. All you need to do is click the left mouse button to start your stress-relieving rampage. Earn coins as you attack and use your earnings to buy new weapons.

Weapons Galore

The game arms you with an arsenal of weapons to choose from. Some of them are as follows:

  • Knife: Fun to throw consecutively.
  • Gun: Quick and direct, for those instant gratification moments.
  • Automatic Rifle: When you want to keep the pressure on.
  • RPG: For those who like to see it all go boom!
  • Rocket: When you need to send your dummy flying.

Each weapon is designed to make your stress relief session as satisfying as possible.

Tips for Puppet Master

  • As you level up by attacking the puppet, you'll earn coins. Spend these wisely to unlock new weapons. Starting with just your fists, soon you'll be throwing cars at your dummy!
  • Don't just stick to one backdrop. Use your coins to unlock different accesories for puppet.
  • Puppet Master will be your personal stress relief buddy from now on. Every click, every hit, every explosion is designed to let you unwind, laugh, and maybe even feel a bit better about your day. So, when life gets too much, step into the world of Puppet Master, where your only task is to let loose and enjoy the chaos.

Controls

  • Left Click: Attack the puppet.

What is Puppet Master?

Puppet Master is a stress-relief action game where players can use various weapons to attack a robotic dummy, providing a fun way to vent frustration.

What kind of weapons does Puppet Master offer?

The game includes a range from melee weapons like swords and hammers to ranged weapons like guns and rockets, even extending to tanks for a unique twist.

How do you advance in Puppet Master?

By attacking the dummy, players earn coins which can be used to unlock new weapons and skins.
