What is FreezeNova?

Our mission drives us to continually develop and publish fun and engaging games for Android, iOS, Chromebooks, and Windows.

Diverse Game Portfolio

One of FreezeNova's most popular games is Masked Forces, a browser-based multiplayer FPS game. Upon its initial release in 2016, Masked Forces captivated. millions of online players and was published by many gaming websites and stores, including Steam and Xbox.

The game's popularity led to the creation of a successful series of sequels, further solidifying its place in the online gaming trends.

While FreezeNova first gained publicity with a multiplayer shooter game, we've continued to create and offer fun games in diverse genres and operating systems.

FreezeNova's games, which are compatible with various devices, have been played by millions of gamers around the world so far. HTML5 games (playable on both mobile and computers) and WEB-GL games with detailed 3D graphics have provided a wide range of gaming experiences to suit every taste.
