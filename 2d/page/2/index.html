Police Chase Drifter

Get your wheels on and beat the cops in a high speed chase that has frills and spills at every turn. Make the police cars bump and slide and makes sure that they fall behind. You’ll have challenges to complete while collecting coins and upgrading your vehicle to ensure that you stay one step ahead of the game. Stickman Ragdoll

If you want to play with physics, you could have some crazy fun with Stickman Ragdoll. Throw him around, make him jump and get a few bruises in by finding the perfect combination of moves for funny results. It’s a great stress reliever with multiple levels and a high score, you’ll find yourself coming back, even if you’re not entirely sure why!

Hero Dragon Power

Time for a classic platformer now with Hero Dragon Power; this game combines the gameplay you know and love with some familiar-looking characters. Your team will consist of two heroes who will help you to traverse the magical world full of creatures and foes; as the game’s hero, evil forces should be quaking in their boots when you unleash your unlockable dragon powers. You can choose between fire breath, lightning strikes and ice blasts to battle your enemies, solve puzzles and collect power-ups along the way. It's not going to be a walk in the park, as 20 levels will get progressively harder as you play - and some really will require skill, concentration and maybe even a little bit of strategy to complete. Speed and power are the name of the game as you have to get through each level without losing your three lives. You can switch between hero pairs if you feel that your initial team isn't quite living up to expectations.

Draw the Bridge

When you want some arcade fun, Draw the Bridge has the simple game mechanic of drag and draw to get the vehicle from A to B. The are 30 levels of drawing fun that will require logic and precision, but things will likely get frustrating. That isn’t to say that this isn’t a highly entertaining game that will keep you coming back.

Slither Snakes

Do you love bright, neon graphics combined with simple, intuitive gameplay? Well, the good news is that Slither Snakes will tick all of the right boxes. You will navigate around, chomping shiny balls of light - and if you bump heads with an opponent, they will drop a collection for you to gobble up too! How long can you get your sake before you die?

Nova Idle Clicker

You can’t talk about 2D games without mentioning a cool take on a classic like Nova Idle Clicker, which is based on old school pong. You’ll simply need to hit the ball off the paddle and watch it get rid of the blocks as it bounces. With super cool neon graphics, upgrades and bonuses to make things even more fun - it’s simple to see why this game is addictive and well-loved.

Mad Doctor

In the same vein as Stickman Ragdoll, the doctor is in the house ready for you to dismantle him. Nobody likes the Mad Doctor, so why not give him a smash? They say an apple a day keeps the doctor away, but why not try throwing stars and syringes instead? You can level up to unlock more weapons, including these and more by earning coins as you go.

Galactic Driver

There comes a time in everybody’s day to escape from the world and into the cosmos - and Galactic Driver is here to elevate things to new heights. Test the capabilities of your sleek new vehicle as you power along the galactic highway, but remember, sometimes safety will get you further than tricking out. Be patient and complete all 30 levels to beat the game - but be warned, this won’t be a simple feat. Are you ready to take on the challenge?
