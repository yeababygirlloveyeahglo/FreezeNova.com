The top unblocked arcade games

Basketball Slam Dunk

If you are looking for a classic basketball game with pixel graphics, 2D gameplay and two player functionality when you want to play with a friend, Basketball Slam Dunk is a great arcade game for you. You can play alongside AI or you can go head to head against your friends - all you need to do is perform slam dunk after slam dunk to win.

StackBall.io

Arcade games don't come more challenging than StackBall.io, where you'll have no choice but to control a jittery, jumpy little ball and get it to the bottom of the helix to win the game. All you'll have to do is tap your mouse to control the ball and smash through the colored parts of the block - but don't forget to avoid the black platforms, as these mean sudden and instant death.

Tunnel

Do you want a virtue adventure that's going to keep you entertained for hours on end? If so, Tunnel can really test your metal as you roll your ball endlessly through this convoluted, unpredictable path, collect the crystals, jump over the holes and be sure not to get caught up. With a point scoring system, you’ll certainly want to get a high score every single time you play.

Hook

If you're looking for a more casual arcade game with simple graphics but plenty of fun, Hook is here to bring you 50 levels of great graphics with a secret mechanic that you'll have to work out to be able to progress. Your cute little character will balance its way around the axis - all you'll need to do is grab onto a hook and click your way around; it really is as simple as that.

American Touchdown

If American football is more your bag, it may be time to take a look at American Touchdown. You can play in single or two-player modes (when you play on your own, you will also be able to choose whether to play against AI opponents). Much like Basketball Slam Dunk, there are some great pixel graphics that will keep you entertained whenever you need a few minutes of mindless fun.

Stickman Ragdoll

Stickman Ragdoll is one of the funniest titles on the whole of FreezeNova, as users simply pick up this cute little Stickman and spend their time trying to ruin his life. The aim of the game is to put him in an array of positions where he can fall down and gain as many injuries as possible. Get creative and unleash your wild side as you bash him about for nothing more than a few laughs.

Puppet Killer

Keeping in the same vein as Stickman now, this arcade game has the slightly more brutal name of Puppet Killer, but those who like a little bit of destruction will have maximum fun. With a little Sackboy looking puppet hanging on a stage, simply point and click to throw knives at him - you’ll collect coins and cause maximum damage as you do. Each hit will give you a reward and these will add up so that you’ll be able to unlock a whole host of weapons including laser guns, rocket launchers, flame throwers and more. Tapping games are certainly on the rise for some of the more fun titles in 2023, so why not kill that little voodoo doll and relieve some stress whenever you've got a few minutes to spare?

Draw the Bridge

For a game that requires a little more skill and strategy, it's time for Draw the Bridge to step into the fray. The aim of the game is to draw a path for an array of different vehicles and there are 30 levels to keep you on your toes. As you have to drag and draw, this game is probably better played on phones or tablets - but it can still be played on the PC by clicking and dragging the mouse. Will you be able to save the vehicle passengers or will they fall down holes and more? Each level has a star rating, so why not see if you can get all three on every single level?

Fall Heroes Knockout

Not all unblocked games are created equal and when it comes to fast-paced, high-octane action; Fall Heroes Knockout is one of the most challenging 3D titles you'll find around the web. With stunning levels, brilliant graphics and astounding animations, you will go against robots and other players to really test your metal. Will you be the last man standing?
