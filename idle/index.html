What Makes Idle Games So Popular?

Idle games (sometimes also called “incremental games”) are a type of clicker games that require minimal player interaction, and the fundamental mechanic of the game is basically “watching numbers go bigger”. The gameplay is centered on gaming some form of in-game currency or resource, usually with the beginning involving clicking or tapping a button to gain them, and later automating the process with various upgrades.

This eventually leads to exponential growth, and the player’s role becomes more about managing and optimizing these gains. The games are often straightforward to understand and play, and give the players a very rewarding sense of progression with time.
