Super Mini Racing

By FreezeNova

Explore the world of miniature car racing for an experience that blends fun with competition. Focus on mastering each level's challenges, from jumps to drifts. Succeed all levels with precise control over your vehicle's speed and handling. Unlock new cars and challenges as you decide between solo or multiplayer modes. Enjoy the visuals and music that make each race engaging and memorable. Get ready to start your engines and compete for victory in Super Mini Racing!

Developer

Super Mini Racing is developed by FreezeNova.

Controls

Single Player or Player 1

  • Steer: WASD keys
  • Respawn: R

Player 2

  • Steer: Arrow keys
  • Respawn: M

The Basics

  • Choose from 10 unique maps and set the difficulty level to easy, medium, or hard.
  • Drive your chosen vehicle through 10 challenging levels, competing against AI opponents or a friend in split-screen mode.
  • Use the arrow keys to drive and steer, and WASD keys along with arrow keys for split-screen mode.

How to Play

  • Earn 1,000 coins by finishing in first place and unlock new levels.
  • Perform impressive stunts like drifts and airtime to multiply your earnings.
  • The more daring your stunt, the more coins you'll earn!
  • Practice makes perfect - get comfortable with your vehicle and the tracks.
  • Don't be afraid to try new cars - experimentation can lead to big rewards!
