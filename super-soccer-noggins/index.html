Super Soccer Noggins

By FreezeNova

Calling all soccer fans! Are you ready to kick off an epic adventure with Super Soccer Noggins, the unblocked sports game that lets you represent your favorite national team on the pitch?

Choose Your Team

Select from nine countries, including Poland, Russia, England, Spain, Italy, Portugal, Germany, France, and the Netherlands. Each country has its own unique footballer with skills and strengths to match. Which team will you choose?

Controls

Player 1

  • Up Arrow: Jump
  • Left-Right Arrows: Steer Left and Right
  • Down Arrow: Kick

Player 2

  • W: Jump
  • A-D Keys: Steer Left and Right
  • S: Kick

How to PlayÂ Super Soccer Noggins

Steer your player from left to right and kick the ball to your opponent's goal when the moment is right. Be the top scorer in each match and tournament, and make your name shine in gold! You can play against AI-controlled opponents or challenge your friend in Local 2 Players mode.

Tournaments Ahead

Take part in exciting tournaments like the Le Croissant Cup, Pinata Trophy, Hei Tiki Taka Cup, Golden Skull Cup, Ruby Merman Cup, and Hellfire Trophy. Each tournament has eight matches, and you'll need to be the top scorer to win. The more you win, the closer you'll get to claiming the cup!

Local 2 Players

Gather a friend and challenge each other on various football pitches. In Local 2 Players mode, you'll face off against each other in thrilling matches. Choose your teams and let the competition begin!
